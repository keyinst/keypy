Mean models obtaind from 61 male, healthy, student participants (aged 18-32) across four eyes closed conditions (7* resting runs, 6* object visualization runs, 6* spatial visualization runs, and 6 verbalization runs à 50 s each).

Milz P, Faber PL, Lehmann D, Koenig T, Kochi K, Pascual-Marqui RD (2016). The functional significance of EEG microstates - Associations with modalities of thinking. Neuroimage, 125:643-56.
